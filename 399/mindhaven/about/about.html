About us
Our mission is to provide valuable information about Mental health and wellness to empower individuals to make informed decisions about their health. We aim to raise awareness about common health issues, share practical tips for maintaining good mental health, and provide resources for further information and support.
We would like to thank Ma'am Charity Nice Nulo for sharing her insights and knowledge about mental health awareness. Her expertise on being the guidance councelor in our school were invaluable in completing this project. We are very grateful for her time and dedication to this project.
