So it's very important to know the self first because some of us become hesitant to share, it's better to open up themselves, find the trust to share their inner self. But the problem is they find it hard to look for someone who they can trust with their feelings. Hence they tend to keep it to themselves which can create or carry a burden to them.The importance of mental health to students is to improve our life as a student, creates good relationship with parents friends, and to become more capable and develop their thoughts.
5 most common mental health issues that students experienced
1. Stress
Students may experience various types of stress, including academic stress, social stress, financial stress. High levels of stress can negatively impact mental and physical health, leading to anxiety, depression, and other mental health issues.
2. Financial
Financial problems can be a significant source of stress for students, particularly those who are managing student loans, working part-time jobs, or struggling to make ends meet. Financial stress can also impact students' ability to focus on their studies, leading to academic challenges.
3. Workload
Students may struggle with managing their workload, particularly during busy perios of the academic year. This can lead to feelings of overwhelm, burnout, and academic pressure, which can negatively impact mental health.
4.Lack of Sense of Belongingness
Students who feel disconnected from their peers, campus community, or social support networks may experience a lack of sense of belongingness. This can lead to feeling of loneliness, isolation, and anxiety, and can negatively impact mental health and academic performance.
5. Family Problems
Family problems, such as conflict, illness, or financial stress, can impact students' mental wellbeing. Students who are dealing with family problems may experience anxiety, depression, or other mental health issues, which can make it difficult to focus on their studies or maintain healthy relationships.
5 Tips That Can Help to Improve Mental Health
1. Read books that can help you
Reading books can be an effective way to improve mental health by reducing stress, promoting relaxation, and increasing self-awareness. Books on topics such as mindfulness, self-help, and personal growth can be particularly beneficial.
2. Be involve
Being involved in activities that bring a sense of purpose and meaning can improve mental health. This can include volunteering, joining a club or organization, or participationg in hobbies and interests.
3. Be open and ask for help
Being open about mental health struggles and seeking help when neded is an important step in improving mental health. This can include talking to friends and family, seeking professional help, or joining support groups.
4. Find your hobby
Engaging in hobbies and activites that bring joy and relaxation can improve mental health by reducing stress and promoting a sense of well-being. This can include anything from reading and writing to exercise and creative pursuits.
5. Meditate
Meditation and mindfulness practices can be effective in reducing stress and promiting relaxation. Regular meditation can also improve mental clarity, focus, and emotional regulation.
